# Case Study – Exploring Customer Behavior

## Project Overview

This SQL project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior using sales and membership data. The objective was to identify customer preferences, product performance, revenue trends, and the impact of membership programs on customer engagement.

Using SQL queries, the project explores customer transactions, product popularity, revenue generation, and gold membership adoption to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Project Objective

* Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
* Identify top-selling and most profitable products.
* Evaluate the impact of gold membership on customer behavior.
* Measure customer loyalty and engagement.
* Generate business insights to support marketing and retention strategies.

---

## Dataset Overview

The analysis was performed using multiple relational tables containing:

* Customer Information
* Sales Transactions
* Product Details
* Gold Membership Data
* User Signup Information

The dataset represents customer orders, product purchases, membership activity, and revenue generation.

---

## Key Findings

* Butter Chicken generated the highest sales revenue.
* Gold members demonstrated stronger purchase loyalty.
* Some customers significantly increased purchases after becoming gold members.
* Not all early users upgraded to the membership program.
* A small group of products contributed a large share of total revenue.

---

## Business Insights

### Product Performance

Top-selling products contribute the majority of revenue and should remain a focus for marketing and promotional campaigns.

### Customer Loyalty

Gold members exhibit stronger engagement and repeat purchasing behavior compared to non-members.

### Membership Growth

Opportunities exist to increase membership adoption through targeted promotions and customer incentives.

### Revenue Optimization

Understanding customer purchasing behavior can help improve recommendations, promotions, and retention strategies.

---

## Recommendations

* Promote high-performing products through targeted campaigns.
* Increase awareness of gold membership benefits.
* Reward loyal customers with personalized offers.
* Use first-purchase behavior to improve recommendation strategies.
* Monitor customer lifecycle trends to improve retention.
